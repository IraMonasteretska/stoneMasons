ABOUT US

We are
Master Stonemasons

Master Stonemasons is Ireland’s leading supplier of stone masonry labour, products and fitting solutions.

Set up in 2003 we provide quality stonemasonry, stone works and restoration works to private, residential and commercial stone building projects.

We have a proven track record in Ireland and the UK, having completed large-scale housing and restoration projects from the design process to supply and fit. We have built a network of strong relationships with local quarries and producers of stone products world-wide to ensure we only use the highest-grade stone in our projects.

Master Stonemasons has maintained its position as Ireland’s leading stone masonry company by offering our expert knowledge and a quality service to its clients. Our competitive edge is our large, experienced, workforce.
